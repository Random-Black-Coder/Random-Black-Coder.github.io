No Show Prediction AI App

I trained a TensorFlow Artificial Neural Network (ANN) using TensorFlow and patient data that could predict the likelihood of a patient being a no-show for their medical appointment. The model had an accuracy of 72%. I deployed the model in a flask web application, I then hosted the application on Heroku. The model will allow medical staff to better identify which patients are likely to miss their appointment and give them the opportunity be proactive in solving this problem.
